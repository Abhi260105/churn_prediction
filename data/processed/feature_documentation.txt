======================================================================
FEATURE ENGINEERING DOCUMENTATION
======================================================================

Features Created:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. TenureGroup: New/Growing/Loyal based on tenure
2. TenureGroup_Numeric: Numeric encoding of tenure groups
3. IsNewCustomer: Binary flag for customers <=6 months
4. IsLoyalCustomer: Binary flag for customers >=36 months
5. TenureYears: Tenure converted to years
6. ServiceCount: Total number of add-on services
7. HasPremiumServices: Has security or backup services
8. HasStreamingServices: Has TV or Movie streaming
9. IsFullBundle: Has both phone and internet
10. InternetType_Numeric: Numeric encoding of internet type
11. ChargePerTenure: Average monthly charge (TotalCharges/tenure)
12. IsHighCharges: Monthly charges > Rs100.91
13. LowValuePerception: High charges but few services
14. PaymentRiskFlag: Manual payment method (not automatic)
15. IsMonthToMonth: Month-to-month contract (high churn risk)
16. HasLongContract: Annual or biennial contract
17. ChargeServiceRatio: Monthly charges per service
18. ContractType_Numeric: Numeric encoding of contract type
19. IsAutoPayment: Automatic payment method
20. RiskScore: Composite risk score (0-11, higher=more risk)
21. RiskCategory: Risk level: Low/Medium/High

======================================================================
Feature Categories:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Tenure & Lifecycle: TenureGroup, IsNewCustomer, IsLoyalCustomer
2. Usage Behavior: ServiceCount, HasPremiumServices, IsFullBundle
3. Billing & Payment: ChargePerTenure, PaymentRiskFlag, IsMonthToMonth
4. Risk Scoring: RiskScore, RiskCategory
